Media Player
This is a simple tool that allows you to play songs (WAV or MP3 audio files) located on your computer.  Load visualizers to see how your songs are visualized!
1.  Song Title
2.  Artist Name and Album Title
3.  Play/Pause Button - Click or toggle with SPACEBAR
4.  Progress Bar - Current location in the track. Click to skip to the desired track time
5.  Load Track Button - Click to bring up a load file menu. Browse to the desired song and select
6.  Stop Button - Stop playing and unload the track
7.  Loop Track Button - Click to toggle track looping. The button will appear red when the track is looping
